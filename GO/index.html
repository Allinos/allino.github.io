Number of Internet Users Per Country , 2019

Nowadays, the world cannot be imagined without the Internet. We Are Social (2019) reports that the number of Internet users worldwide is growing at a remarkable rate, with nearly 900,000 people going online every day for the first time since July 2018. A year before that, 46.8% of the global population used the Internet, which is expected to increase to 53.7% in 2021 (eMarketer).

Currently, six out of every ten of the world's population have internet access (Obero, 2019). The number of Internet users in 2019 increased by 327 million year-on-year compared to figures for the third quarter of 2018. This indicates an increase of 8.2% in active Internet users worldwide, which is eight times faster than the overall population growth. . which is at 1% (Obero, 2019).

What is the number of Internet Users in 2021?

The number of Internet users in the world is increasing rapidly. Experts report an increase of 1,157% in the number of Internet users from 2000-2019 (We Are Social, 2019). In addition, as of January 2021, there are 4.66 billion Internet users worldwide (DataReportal, 2021). A worldwide average penetration rate of 59.5% was recorded.

With a quarterly growth of 32 million Internet subscribers (5%) and an annual growth of 143 million subscribers (29%) (The Economic Times, 2019), India continues to deliver the largest absolute growth.
India's neighbors are also reporting promising growth, although southern Asia still has the largest unaffiliated population, with more than a billion people across the region still waiting to come online.

Social Media Penetration an it's impact on marketing and sale in 2021

There are approximately 4.20 billion active social media users worldwide as of January 2021 (DataReportal, 2021). This is 53.6% of the total world population.
Facebook is still at the forefront of the social media race in both social networks and instant messaging. Similarly, both Facebook and Instagram lead the social network pack, although YouTube, a social network and video platform, currently has around 900 million more users than Instagram.
